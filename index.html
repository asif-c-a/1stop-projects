About

The DeLorean Time Machine. A modified DMC-12 capable of travelling through time. Documented through a seried of 3 motion pictures.

The DeLorean Time Machine is capable of going to any point in time given enough speed (approximately 88mph, or 142 kmph) is maintained throughout the journey. Being a modified version of DeLorean Motor Company's DMC-12, it sports a stylish futuristic-retro look paired with a reliable chassis holding all the required equipped for time travel. A Flux Capacitor, and a Plutonium Powered Reactor are the main elements for Time Travel. It is stacked with features like Gull-Wing Doors, Vents, and Flight Thrusters. It also stored high tech gadgets like the HooverBoard, the Nike Air Mag 'Self Tying Shoes' and Smart Glasses.
